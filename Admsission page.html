Admission Requirements

Welcome to the Computer Science Department at the University of Ghana. Below are the admission requirements for our programs:

Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science

The University of Ghana announces for the information of the general public that applications are open for the admission of prospective applicants into various undergraduate programmes for the 2023/2024 academic year. Applicants should take note of the following: GHANA WASSCE/SSSCE - Minimum/General Entry Requirements General Entry Requirements into Legon Campus, Accra City Campus and Korle Bu Campus. Master's Degree in Computer Science

  • Completion of a relevant bachelor's degree with a minimum GPA
  • Submission of transcripts and letters of recommendation
  • Statement of purpose outlining research interests and goals

Ph.D. in Computer Science

  • Completion of a master's degree in a related field
  • Demonstrated research experience and publications
  • Submission of a research proposal

Course Delivery

Our courses are designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of computer science concepts and applications. The delivery methods include:

  1. Lectures by experienced faculty members
  2. Hands-on laboratory sessions
  3. Project work and assignments
  4. Guest lectures from industry experts
